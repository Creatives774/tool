Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools completely free to use?

Yes, all our basic tools are completely free to use. We offer premium features for power users, but the core functionality is available at no cost.

Is my data secure when using your tools?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your files are processed entirely in your browser or on secure servers and are never stored longer than necessary to complete the operation.

What file formats do you support?

We support all major image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF) and PDF files. Some tools also support less common formats.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you can use most of our tools without creating an account. An account is only needed if you want to save your preferences or access advanced features.
